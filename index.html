The Wheely Safe Front Wheel Lock is a small, lightweight, rod-shaped lock that protects the front wheel of your bike from theft.

Being an active group of students from Boston University’s Questrom School of Business, we noticed the absence of a product to properly secure the front wheel to the bike. Being city residents ourselves, we have witnessed our fair share of bike theft and improper bike-locking practices. Together, with our mission in mind, we set out to create an ideal Front Bike Wheel Lock that anyone can use. With our product, we hope to offer an extra measure of security for all safety-conscious bike owners out there. Take care of your front wheel and order the Wheely Safe Lock today!

Our product is… Secure!

Our Wheely Safe Lock cannot be removed without your secret four digit lock code, keeping your wheel locked to your bike no matter what.

Universal!

Our Lock is compatible with any bicycle you will be using in a city, except large dirt bikes.

Convenient!

Our Lock has a passive locking mechanism that allows you to leave your bike outside without any fear of wheel detachment, with no key needed.

Easy!!

he Lock is easy to install, just simply remove the front bike wheel axle, replace it with the Wheely Safe rod shaped lock, and ride.

Values & Mission

While creating our business, we knew we wanted to help parts of the world that we would not be able to reach with our product. We came across World Bicycle Relief, a non-profit organization that donates bicycles to mobilize people across Africa, specifically Kenya, Mozambique, South Africa, South Sudan, Tanzania, Uganda, Zambia, and Zimbabwe. World Bicycle Relief’s goal is to create a world where distance is no longer a barrier to education, healthcare, and economic opportunity. We will be donating 1% of our Wheely Safe Lock sales to World Bicycle Relief to bring more people bikes and better opportunities.
